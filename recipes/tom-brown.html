TOM BROWN

INGREDIENTS (SERVES 1-2)

  • 3 tablespoons Tom Brown mix (roasted corn flour or blend with millet/soybeans/peanuts)
  • 1 cup water (plus more as needed)
  • Pinch of salt (optional)
  • Sugar or honey (to taste)
  • Evaporated milk or powdered milk (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix the flour:
    • In a bowl, mix Tom Brown flour with a bit of water to form a smooth paste (no lumps).
  2. Heat water in a saucepan:
    • Bring 1-1.5 cups of water to boil.
  3. Add the paste:
    • Gradually pour the Tom Brown paste into the boiling water, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook:
    • Keep stirring on low heat for about 5-10 minutes until thick and smooth.
  5. Add extras:
    • Stir in milk, sugar and a pinch of salt.
  6. Serve hot, often with bread or on its own.

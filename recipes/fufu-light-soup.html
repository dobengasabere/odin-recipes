FUFU (CASSAVA & PLANTAIN OR YAM COMBO)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 piece of cassava
  • 2 plantains (or 1 yam, depending on the combo)
  • water

INSTRUCTIONS (BOILING AND POUNDING METHOD)

OPTIONA A: TRADITIONAL (POUNDING)

  1. Peel and cut cassava and plantain into chunks.
  2. Boil together in water until very soft (about 20-30 minutes).
  3. Drain water and transfer to a mortar.
  4. Pound using a pestle
    • Pound cassava and plantain alternately.
    • Wet pestle as needed to smoothen.
    • Continue until elastic and smooth.

OPTION B: BLENDER/FUFU MACHINE METHOD

  1. Follow steps 1-2 above.
  2. Blend or mash with a little warm water.
  3. knead into a smooth, stretchy consistency.
  4. Shape into balls and serve warm.

LIGHT SOUP (GHANAIAN SPICY BROTH)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1-2 lbs goat meat, beef, chicken, or fish
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes
  • 1-2 medium onions
  • 1 thumb-sized piece ginger
  • 3-5 cloves garlic
  • 2-3 pepper (scotch bonnet or kpakpo shito) to taste
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes (maggi or your choice)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Clean and season meat:
    • wash meat and place in a pot.
    • Blend 1 onion, garlic, pepper, salt, and seasoning cube.
    • Pour over meat and steam for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Add tomatoes:
    • Add fresh tomatoes (and tomato paste if using).
    • Pour into the pot with the meat.
  3. Add water:
    • Add enough water to cover meat and allow it to become soup (1-1.5 litres depending on thickness).
  4. Simmer:
    • Let it cook for another 30-40 minutes until meat is tender and soup is slightly reduced.
    • Adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Finish:
    • Add chopped onions or whole small peppers if desired near the end.
    • Simmer for a few more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with fufu.
